Kenya in 12 days !!!

Day Highlights Overnight
Day 1 Arrive in Nairobi - Free Time to Acclimatize Nairobi
Day 2 Giraffe Centre, Visit to Kazuri Beads Factory, Nairobi National Museum & a local lunch at Road House Grill for the most delicious, Kenyan-style BBQ Goat & Chicken! Nairobi
Day 3 Nairobi City Skyline Tour, Guided Shopping at The Maasai Market & local lunch at 3 Dee Restaurant for the best grilled fish in Nairobi - Kenyan-style of course! Nairobi
Day 4 Transfer to Masai Mara National Reserve in time for lunch and an Afternoon Game Drive Maasai Mara
Day 5 Maasai Village Tour & Big Five Wildlife Spotting in Masai Mara Maasai Mara
Day 6 Morning Game Drive (Optional Balloon Safari)
Transfer to Crescent Island in Lake Naivasha for a Relaxing Boat Ride ,Tour & Walking Safari ~ This is Truly a Bird Watcher’s Paradise! 		Naivasha
Day 7 Relaxing morning enjoying the amazing hotel in Naivasha Ziplining Experience at The Forest while en route back to Nairobi Nairobi
Day 8 Morning Flight to The Coast ;Top beaches incl. Lamu, Diani, Kilifi, Watamu, Malindi & Zanzibar Coast
Day 9 Full Day of Relaxation at the Kenyan Coast Coast
Day 10 Guided Exploration Day ~ Watamu Turtle Watch, Mamba Village, Old Town Tour, Ngulini Wildlife Sanctuary, Spice Markets, Bombolulu Workshop, Vasco da Gama Pillar, Haller Park, Marine Park Visit, Kipepeo Butterfly Project, fresh seafood meal by the water et al. (select 2-6 options) Coast
Day 11 BEACH DAY 🏖️ ~ Watersports, Day Sails, Sunset Dhow Ride et al. Coast
Day 12 Transfer back to Nairobi in time for Return Flight Home Nairobi
