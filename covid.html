Our Commitment to safety

COVID-19-related Commitment to Safety

We are committed to global standard safety and cleaning guidelines. These guidelines were created based on publicly available COVID-19 related guidance from the World Health Organization and the US Centers for Disease Control. Here’s what The African Thrillist Ecotours & Travel Agency has committed to:
• Practicing good hygiene
• Follow hand washing guidelines and ensure all participants wear a protective face covering
• Avoiding physical contact
• Practice social distancing as recommended by the relevant local authority
