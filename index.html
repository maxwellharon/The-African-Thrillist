What Our Clients Say About Us

“THIS EXPERIENCE!!! My husband and I were on a short layover in Nairobi and are so blessed to have met Raisa. She was great communicating with us, flexible and knowledge about Kenya. We really enjoyed learning about the Kenyan culture by walking through the streets and experiencing the architecture, people, food (roadhouse grill...I can still taste the food) and the arts & crafts in the market. We were able to get some great deals on souvenirs to take back home. Please book this experience worth every penny! ”

— Latanya, San Diego, CA

“This is a wonderful way to orient yourself in Nairobi. Great views of the skyline, a walk through Central Park, good local food and awesome shopping! Raisa is warm, knowledgeable and passionate about her city (and country). She's also exceptional at getting the best deals at the market! I would recommend this experience to anyone visiting.”

— Orchid, Nassau, The Bahamas

“Raisa was an amazing host, everything we ate was delicious. She responded to all of my demands. She made it easy for me to visit a side of Nairobi that I would not have been able to see on my own. If you're looking for an amazing food tour, Raisa will be the perfect host for you! Also do not hesitate to ask her to personalize your tour, she is very flexible and attentive to your requests. ”

— Nadia, Gatineau, Canada

“Raisa’s presence, power, energy and aura make this experience special and unique. From the beginning to end she catered the tour specifically to meet my interests. She provides tremendous personal life perspective on the history and culture of Nairobi, Kenya and the continent of Africa. She encompasses both a local and global perspective on the world. Some of the highlights of the tour were shopping with her at the Masai market in which she’s particularly skilled at bargaining and will get you the best possible price and the traditional Kenyan dinner we shared in which I felt like I was dining with one of my best friends! In fact, her caring and genuine presence made it easy to befriend this wonderful human. I highly recommend this extremely authentic experience.”

— Deonisis, New York, NY

“This was a wonderful experience! Willis, my guide was very kind and friendly and spent a really long time with me teaching me about Kenyan culture and telling me about his experiences growing up here. We were friends by the time we parted! The giraffes were sweet and amazing and ate out of our hands. The bead factory was really interesting and I purchased some very unique items. Overall, I highly recommend it!”

— Cassandra, Vancouver, Canada

“Excellent guide! One-of-a-kind experience! Raisa's professionalism, knowledge of Nairobi, and hospitality were unmatched. The overall experience and value of this tour were top tier.”

“Fantastic experience and a great way to experience the Kenyan nightlife. I was traveling solo and I felt so comfortable with Raisa - she made me feel welcomed and safe. Raisa was super accommodating (from preferences to dietary restrictions), professional and provided some great insights. Definitely recommend it!”
